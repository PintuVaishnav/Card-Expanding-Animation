Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is a fictional character in the Naruto manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. He is one of the main characters in the series and is a member of the Uchiha clan, a prestigious ninja family in the Hidden Leaf Village (Konohagakure).

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha, a pivotal character in the Naruto series, hailed from the esteemed Uchiha clan, known for his prodigious ninja skills. His background is marked by a tragic incident where he was tasked with the unthinkable: the annihilation of his entire clan, including his parents, sparing only his younger brother, Sasuke.

Madara Uchiha

Madara, the co-founder of the Uchiha Clan and played a significant role in the history of the Naruto world. He was a skilled and powerful ninja who possessed the Sharingan and later the Rinnegan, two of the most advanced and potent dojutsu (eye techniques) in the series.
